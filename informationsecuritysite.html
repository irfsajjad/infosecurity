Overview

Information security refers to the protection of digital information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction.

Risks

There are many risks associated with information security, including:

  • Data breaches
  • Malware infections
  • Phishing attacks
  • Insider threats

Best Practices

To improve information security, it's important to follow these best practices:

  1. Use strong, unique passwords
  2. Enable two-factor authentication
  3. Keep software up to date
  4. Be cautious of suspicious emails and links
  5. Regularly back up important data
